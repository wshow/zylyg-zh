It is harder than aluminum oxide grit and almost as hard as valuable stones on the Mohs scale. Because of these qualities, it is a very potent medium. Compared to silicon carbide powders of lesser purity, silicon carbide particles are treated at higher temperatures. The use of silicon carbide grit abrasive paper is the most efficient and practical method for degrading metallic metallographic models.
